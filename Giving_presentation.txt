## Giving Business Presentation

✅ 1. Starting the Presentation

Good morning/afternoon everyone

Thank you for joining us today

I’m pleased to be here

Today I’d like to talk about…

The purpose of this presentation is to…

My focus today will be on…

✅ 2. Structuring / Signposting

These phrases help you guide the audience:

First of all…

To begin with…

Let’s move on to…

Next, I’d like to discuss…

Now, turning to…

Finally…

In summary…

To conclude…

✅ 3. Presenting Facts & Data

According to our data…

The figures show that…

We observed a significant increase/decrease in…

The trend indicates…

Our projections suggest…

Statistically speaking…

This resulted in…

This impacted our revenue/operations by…

✅ 4. Highlighting Key Points

It’s important to note that…

Let me emphasize…

The key takeaway here is…

What’s critical here is…

I would like to stress that…

Another crucial point is…

✅ 5. Explaining Ideas

In other words…

To put it simply…

What this means is that…

Let me clarify…

This refers to…

✅ 6. Comparing & Contrasting

Compared to last year…

As opposed to…

In contrast…

On the other hand…

Similarly…

Likewise…

✅ 7. Persuasive Business Language

Useful for product pitches / strategy presentations:

We believe this approach will…

This strategy enables us to…

This gives us a competitive advantage

This will drive growth in…

We are confident that…

There is strong demand for…

✅ 8. Handling the Audience / Interaction

Feel free to ask questions at any time

I’ll address questions at the end

Let me clarify that for you

Could you please repeat the question?

That’s a great question

Thank you for raising that point

✅ 9. Visual Aids / Slides

As you can see on the slide…

This chart illustrates…

Let’s take a look at this graph

This table highlights…

If we look closely, we notice…

✅ 10. Ending the Presentation

To sum up…

In conclusion…

That brings me to the end of my presentation

Thank you for your attention

I’m happy to take your questions now
