"Be in a bind" (verb phrase) means to have a problem or to be in a difficult situation.

    Examples: She was in a bind because she lost her presentation notes.


"Retrace your steps" (verb phrase) means to revisit or think about the places you have been in order to find something that you lost.

    Examples: Jill found her purse after she retrace her steps

"Quit cold turkey" means to stop a habut suddenly and completely(without slowly reducing it), usually referring to addictions like smoking etc

    Examples:  He quit smoking cold turkey after his health report came in

"Fall off the wagon" means to return to bad habit after quitting it (usually smoking)

    Example: I was off sugar but I fell off the wagon last night.
